Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 03-03-2026

LotManager respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

LotManager does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All vehicle details, images, pricing information, and notes are stored locally on your device.

2. Camera Usage

The app uses the device camera solely to capture vehicle photos for inventory management. Images are stored locally and are not shared externally.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the device using on-device storage. No cloud storage or third-party servers are used.

4. Data Sharing

LotManager does not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties.

5. Security

Because data is stored locally, users are responsible for securing their device with PIN, password, or biometric protection.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for business use and is not directed toward children under 13.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated in the future. Updates will be reflected with a revised effective date.
